K. Davis Senseman

Founder

Davis discovered her passion for helping business owners far before she realized her own entrepreneurial leanings. However, upon realizing her dual interest (and the need to now “practice what she preached”) Davis’s interest in business law matters has only deepened. The ability to understand the needs of business owners and their families allows Davis to consider aspects of client issues that sometimes even they haven’t realized, and creates a relationship beyond attorney/client…for most clients Davis serves a more comprehensive business advisor role. This is one reason Davis Law Office is located within CoCo, the largest co-working space in Minneapolis, so Davis can always find other top-notch service providers no matter what need her clients may have.

Emily Buchholz

Associate

When Emily met Davis for coffee in 2013 it was clear that the two shared the same professional core values: thinking outside the box, adding real value to clients’ lives, fee transparency, and holistic advocacy, to name a few. DLO was ready to add on another attorney, Emily was ready to join forces with the awesome DLO and CoCo energy, and the rest is history! Emily sees herself as “general counsel” for people who happen to be business owners. Most of her practice focuses on transactional business matters, but she also assists clients with family matters, such as estate planning, and litigation advising. Prior to coming on board with DLO, Emily litigated and negotiated family law cases, was trained and worked as a mediator, and clerked for two different judges. Before attending law school at the University of Minnesota, Emily worked at a magazine for a number of years and earned her bachelors degree in English Literature from the University of Wisconsin – Madison. Despite being a lawyer, she is also a creative person. She and her spouse, Mark, have three young kids and a Frenchie named Walter, who calls CoCo his second home.

Joseph Levitt

Associate

Joe brings a breadth of international and local business experience to the firm. Prior to law school, Joe worked in Prague, Czech Republic teaching English at multinational corporations such as Philips, Abbott Laboratories and O2, law firms and local Czech businesses. Beyond improving their employees’ language skills, Joe consulted these companies on marketing programs, community presentations and integrating into English speaking markets. Joe’s first-hand experience as an entrepreneurial business owner, operating a successful language and consulting studio in a foreign country, sets him apart from other attorneys as he understands many of the issues and concerns facing growing businesses.
